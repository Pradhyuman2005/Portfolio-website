Problem Statement

Travellers on Airbnb struggle to find compatible co-travellers for shared stays, often leading to mismatched expectations and uncomfortable experiences. There is no streamlined way to discover and connect with like-minded individuals, making shared accommodation planning uncertain and inefficient.

Features to Include:

1. AI integration to help users decide their co-travellers.
2. A button similar to sliding button to make co-traveller mode intuitive.
3. A well sorted grid that categorises every property.
4. Users will connect to potential travellers first before getting to know personal
details like phone number or social media IDs.

Impact

1. All users found the app intuitive and low-friction, completing tasks smoothly without confusion or errors.
2. The design established strong visual credibility by aligning closely with Airbnb design patterns,
increasing user trust, familiarity, and likelihood of continued use..
3. All users felt the AI-driven matching feature effectively addressed compatibility concerns,
reinforcing the app’s core value and usefulness.
4. All users felt the interface was polished and engaging, with aesthetics and animations enhancing
their overall experience.
5. All users felt encouraged to return to the app, because of the positive experiences and design
quality supporting long-term retention.
